## Overview  

**Unclutter for Mac** is a compact and practical productivity application designed to help users maintain a tidy workspace while keeping important tools at their fingertips. With its versatile drop-down panel, Unclutter allows you to quickly access notes, temporary files, and clipboard history without interrupting your workflow.  

The app enhances macOS functionality with fast drag-and-drop, convenient keyboard shortcuts, and an intuitive interface that makes it easy to manage digital clutter. Users can organize notes, store temporary files, and track clipboard content all in one unified location, helping to streamline day-to-day tasks.  

Unclutter is ideal for professionals, students, and anyone who wants to reduce desktop chaos while improving efficiency. Its lightweight design ensures minimal system load while providing powerful organization capabilities. Fully compatible with **Intel and Apple Silicon Macs**, Unclutter offers smooth, reliable performance and seamless integration with macOS aesthetics.  

Whether managing documents, capturing quick notes, or monitoring clipboard items, **Unclutter** simplifies multitasking, improves productivity, and keeps your desktop clean and organized at all times.
